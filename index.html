how it works?

1

Leave an application on our website. We will reach out to you to answer all your questions.

2

We will appoint an e-meeting with a coach to define your language needs.

3

Based on your expectations and language level, we will compile an individual development program and confirm it with you.

4

We start a course at a time convenient for you.

5

You daily train your skills and receive feedback from your coach.

6

We keep track of your advancement and conduct monthly reflections and analyses of the dynamics of your language development with the academic director of EverEnglish.

7

You take a test you've prepared for, enjoy WOW opportunities, and reach your academic goals.
